2020

Karuna 2020

Karuna 2020 is an effort to try to bring together people and do our part in helping feed those most affected during the coronavirus pandemic. It's a collaboration between startups, corporates, and nonprofits in Delhi–NCR. During the COVID-19 lockdown, we helped feed 10,000+ people and distributed 100,000+ masks to frontline workers.
