2020

Karuna 2020

Karuna 2020 was an effort to try to bring together people and do our part in helping feed those most affected during the coronavirus pandemic. It was a collaboration between startups, corporates, and nonprofits in Delhi–NCR. During the COVID-19 lockdown, we helped feed 10,000+ people with a month's ration and distributed 100,000+ masks to frontline workers.

2017

BharatHacks

BharatHacks was the first-of-its-kind hackathon to solve India-specific problems that will helped participants take their idea forward, from hacking to raising investment to launching products in the market. Over 100 engineers, designers, and entrepreneurs participated on the weekend of June 17–18, organized in collaboration with technology companies like Facebook, DigitalOcean, IBM, IndiaStack, and Aadhaar Bridge.

2013

Made with Love in India

Made with Love in India is an initiative to celebrate, promote, and build a brand — India. Over 100 startups, including Snapdeal and Hike Messenger, use the Made with Love in India badge on their products. The platform helps local entrepreneurs connect with each other and show off Made in India products and services.
